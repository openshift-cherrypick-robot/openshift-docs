= Viewing snoozed CVEs

[role="_abstract"]
Use the *Vulnerability Management* view to see the list of all snoozed CVEs.

.Procedure

. From the *Vulnerability Management* view header, click *CVEs*.
. On the *CVEs* view, click the *View Snoozed CVEs* icon.
